# Backlog

## Purpose
Use this document to collect candidate work items before they are selected for implementation.

## Backlog Item Format
Define the preferred structure for backlog items, such as identifier, title, description, rationale, priority, status, estimate, acceptance criteria, and links.

## Prioritization
Describe how items should be ranked and what factors influence priority.

## Ready Criteria
List the information required before an item is ready for implementation.

## Candidate Items
Capture proposed work items, grouped by theme or priority.

## Technical Debt
Track maintenance, refactoring, documentation, and infrastructure improvements.

## Parking Lot
Store ideas that may be valuable later but are not currently prioritized.
