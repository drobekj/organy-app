# Roadmap

## Purpose
Use this document to communicate planned product direction without over-specifying implementation tasks.

## Roadmap Principles
Describe how roadmap items should be prioritized, reviewed, and adjusted.

## Time Horizons
Organize planned outcomes by suitable horizons, such as now, next, later, or dated milestones.

## Themes
Group related goals into product or technical themes.

## Milestones
Define meaningful checkpoints, target outcomes, and dependencies.

## Dependencies and Risks
Track external dependencies, sequencing constraints, and major risks.

## Change Log
Record significant roadmap updates and the reason for each change.
