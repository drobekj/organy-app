# Product Vision

## Purpose
Briefly describe what this document captures and how it should guide product decisions.

## Problem Statement
Describe the user or organizational problem the product is intended to solve.

## Target Users
List primary and secondary user groups, with short notes about their needs.

## Value Proposition
Summarize the unique value the product should provide.

## Success Outcomes
Define measurable outcomes or qualitative signals that indicate the product is successful.

## Scope Boundaries
Clarify what is in scope, out of scope, and intentionally deferred.

## Assumptions and Risks
Record key assumptions, constraints, and risks that may affect product direction.

## Open Questions
Track unresolved questions that must be answered before or during development.
